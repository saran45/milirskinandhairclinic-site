Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer online consultations?

Yes, we offer tele-derm consults. Use the Book button and choose a date/time; we’ll confirm on WhatsApp.

Which laser devices do you use?

We use FDA-cleared platforms appropriate for a range of skin tones. Your dermatologist will recommend the best option.

Is PRP safe?

PRP uses your own plasma; it’s generally safe when performed by trained clinicians. We screen for contraindications during consult.
